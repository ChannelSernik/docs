**ProcedurePart A: Uploading a Video**

**Step 1: Log In**

1. Open a browser (Google Chrome recommended).
2. Go to the **Channel D website** and sign in with your **email address** and **password**.\
   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa675fd073a4f29f61957.jpeg" alt="Channel D login page" /\>
3. Once signed in, you will land on your **dashboard**.

**Step 2: Navigate to Uploads**

1. From the left-hand navigation menu, click **Uploads**.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa6f7aaad916c4278c9f4.jpeg" alt="Channel D uploads page" /\>
1. You will be redirected to the **Uploads Page**, which displays all previously uploaded videos.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa746aaad916a3978eb19.jpeg" alt="Channel D uploads page" /\>

**Step 3: Start Uploading**

1. In the **upper-right corner** of the Uploads page, click **Upload Media**.
2. From the pop-up options, select **Upload a Video**.
1. \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa746aaad916a3978eb19.jpeg" alt="Channel D uploads page" /\>\
   \
   **Step 4: Select Video File**
   1. A file upload window will appear.
   2. You may:
      - **Drag and drop** the video file into the upload area, OR
      - **Click “Choose File”** to browse your device and select the file.
   3. Supported formats: [insert supported file formats if known, e.g., MP4, MOV].
   4. Wait for the video to upload — progress will be displayed.

   _Best Practice: Keep video file sizes optimized for smooth playback._

   **\
   Step 5: Add Video Details**
   1. After the upload completes, a details form will appear.
   2. Enter the following information:
      - **Title** → e.g., _Dental Tips_ or _Practice Introduction_.
      - **Description** (optional) → add context or notes about the video.
   3. Choose how you want to handle the video:
      - **Add to Playlist** → immediately add the video to an active playlist.
      - **Save for Later** → store the video in your uploads library without publishing.

   **Step 6: (Optional) Schedule the Video**
   1. To set a specific playback schedule:
      - Select a **Start Date** (when the video becomes active).
      - Select an **End Date** (when the video should stop playing).
   2. Confirm settings before saving.

   **Step 7: Confirm Upload**
   1. Once finalized, the uploaded video will appear in your **Uploads section**.
   2. The video will also be visible in **Playlists** (if added immediately).

   **Part B: Managing Uploaded Videos**

   **\
   Step 1: Locate Video Tools**
   1. On the **Uploads page**, find the video you want to manage.
   2. Hover over the video thumbnail.
   3. In the lower-right corner, click the **three-dot icon (**⋮**)**.

   **Step 2: Manage Options**

   From the three-dot menu, you can:
   - **Pause** → temporarily disable playback without deleting the video.
   - **Delete** → permanently remove the video from uploads and playlists.
   - **Edit** → update video title, description, or scheduling details.

   **Step 3: Sort, Filter, and Search**

   On the **top-right corner** of the Uploads page, three icons are available:
   - **Sort** → arrange videos by date, name, or status.
   - **Filter** → display videos by category (e.g., scheduled, active, inactive).
   - **Search** → quickly find a specific video by name or keyword.

   **Part C: Adding Uploaded Videos to Playlists**

   **Step 1: Access Playlist**
   1. From the left-hand navigation menu, click **Playlist**.
   2. Locate the newly uploaded video in your library.

   **Step 2: Add & Arrange Video**
   1. Drag the uploaded video into the **Now Playing section** of your playlist.
   2. Rearrange playback order by dragging and dropping videos into the preferred sequence.
   3. Click **Save Changes**, then **Confirm**.

   **Step 3: Verify Playlist Update**
   1. Ensure the uploaded video appears in the active playlist.
   2. Preview playback order to confirm correct arrangement.

   **Best Practices**
   - Use **clear, descriptive titles** for uploaded videos to simplify organization.
   - **Schedule seasonal or campaign-specific videos** in advance to automate content management.
   - Regularly **review uploads** and remove outdated or irrelevant videos.
   - Keep **videos short and engaging** (2–5 minutes recommended).

   **Key Notes**
   - Uploaded videos are stored in the **Uploads library** and can be reused in multiple playlists.
   - Scheduling ensures videos only play during the intended timeframe.
   - Uploads are **practice-specific** and do not automatically sync across other practices.
