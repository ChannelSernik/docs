description: "This guide walks you through setting up your LG Smart TV, installing the Channel D app, entering your Practice ID, and ensuring smooth, uninterrupted video playback."
---

## **Initial LG TV Setup**

### **Step 1: Begin Setup Process**

- Press **OK** on your LG remote to start the setup.
- Select your preferred **language**.

### **Step 2: Connect to Wi-Fi**

- Choose the strongest available **Wi-Fi network**.

### **Step 3: Accept Terms and Enable Auto Updates**

- Agree to LG’s **Terms and Conditions**.
- Ensure **Automatic Updates** are turned **ON**.

### **Step 4: Sign in to LG Account**

- Sign in or create an **LG Account**.
- Click **Done** when finished.

---

## **Install and Launch Channel D**

### **Step 5: Open the Apps Section**

- On the Home Screen, select the **Apps** section.

### **Step 6: Search for Channel D**

- Type **“Channel D”** in the search bar\
  _(make sure there is a space between “Channel” and “D”)_.
- Select **Channel D** from the results.

### **Step 7: Install the App**

- Click **Install**.
- The Channel D app will now appear on your Home Screen.

### **Step 8: Enter Your Practice ID**

- Open the Channel D app.
- Enter your **6-digit Practice ID**\
  (found at the top left of your Channel D dashboard).

### **Step 9: Confirm Video Playback**

- Your Channel D videos should now begin playing.

### **Step 10: Move Channel D for Easy Access**

- Reposition the Channel D app icon to a convenient spot on your Home Screen.

---

## **Prevent Sleep Mode Interruptions**

### **Step 11: Turn Off Energy Saving**

- Go to: **Home Screen \> System \> General \> Energy Saving**.
- Set **Energy Saving** to **OFF**.

### **Step 12: Why It Matters**

- Prevents the TV from sleeping.
- Ensures uninterrupted Channel D video playback.

---

## **Troubleshooting**

### **Step 13: Reinstall If Needed**

- If the app misbehaves or an update fails:
  - Select the Channel D app.
  - Press **OK**.
  - Choose **Delete**.

### **Step 14: Reinstall Channel D**

- Go back to the **Apps** section.
- Search **Channel D**.
- Download and reinstall.
