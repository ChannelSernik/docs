---
title: "How to Prevent Your Roku TV from Auto-Sleeping During Channel D Videos Playlist"
description: "If your Roku TV goes into auto-sleep mode while streaming Channel D videos, it could be due to specific settings that manage power, screensavers, or bandwidth. Follow these simple steps to make sure your viewing experience remains uninterrupted."
---

**Step 1: Turn Off the Screen Saver**

Ensure the screen saver does not start while watching your favorite videos.

- Navigate to Settings.
- Go to Screen Saver Start Time.
- Set it to NONE.

**Step 2: Disable Auto Power Savings**

Prevent your TV from powering down due to inactivity:

- Open Settings.
- Select System \> Power \> Auto Power Savings.
- Untick the option After 20 minutes of no interaction.

**Step 3: Turn Off Bandwidth Saver**

Stop the TV from conserving bandwidth when no activity is detected:

- Head to Settings
- Choose Network \> Bandwidth Saver
- Set this option to OFF
- Why These Settings Matter

These adjustments ensure your Roku TV stays active during extended viewing sessions, especially for long-running or static-content videos like those on Channel D. Enjoy uninterrupted streaming!

If issues persist even after changing these settings, consider restarting your Roku TV or reaching out to Roku Support for assistance.
