---
title: "How to Uninstall & Re-install Channel D App on Amazon Fire TV"
description: "This guide explains how to remove an outdated Channel D app from your Amazon Fire TV, reinstall the latest version, and reconnect it using your Practice ID."
---

## **How to Uninstall the Existing Channel D App**

### **Step 1: Go to the Fire TV Home Page**

- Turn on your TV and go to the **Amazon Fire TV home screen**.

### **Step 2: Open Settings**

- Navigate to the **Settings** (cog icon).
- Select **Applications**.

### **Step 3: Manage Installed Applications**

- Select **Manage Installed Applications**.
- Scroll through the list and find **Channel D**.

### **Step 4: Uninstall Channel D**

- Press the **menu (☰)** button on your remote.
- Select **Uninstall** to remove the app.

---

## **How to Reinstall and Connect Channel D**

### **Step 5: Open Amazon Appstore**

- Return to the **Fire TV home screen**.
- Open the **Appstore**.

### **Step 6: Search for Channel D**

Use any of the following search methods:

- Voice command
- On-screen keyboard
- Scan a QR code with your phone

Search for: **“Channel D”**

### **Step 7: Download Channel D**

- Click on the **Channel D** thumbnail.
- Press the round download button (**Get**) to install the latest version.

### **Step 8: Launch the App**

- Open the Channel D app once installed.
- Follow the on-screen prompts to enter your **Practice ID**.

---

## **Confirm Successful Connection**

### **Step 9: Verify Device Connection**

- Visit [**my.channeld.com**](http://my.channeld.com).
- Confirm the status shows **“Device Connected”** with a green dot.

### **Step 10: Check Email Confirmation**

- You will receive an automatic email confirming a successful device connection.
