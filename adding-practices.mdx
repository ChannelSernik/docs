---
title: "Adding Multiple Practices to a Corporate Account in Channel D"
description: "This knowledgebase outlines the process for corporate account holders to add and manage multiple dental practices within Channel D."
---

\*\*Step-by-step Procedure: \*\*

**1. Log In**

1. Go to the **Channel D website**.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/6931974cf3fe2a115371f10b.jpeg" alt="Channel D login page" /\>
3. Click **Sign In** to access your dashboard.

**2. Access Practice Management**

3. On the dashboard, locate the **drop-down menu** in the upper-left corner displaying your current practice name.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/6931977581eaa1dcd073327f.jpeg" alt="Channel D Dashboard" /\>
2. Click the drop-down.
3. Select **Add a New Practice**.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/693197b81d466e41a09e1837.jpeg" alt="Channel D Dashboard" /\>

**Add a New Practice**

4. A pop-up form will appear.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/693197fce0f0925b208f5058.jpeg" alt="Channel D Dashboard" /\>
2. Enter the following required details:
   - **Email Address** (for practice login and notifications)
   - **Practice Name**
   - **Contact Name** (primary contact for that practice)
3. Click **Create Practice**.

**Free Trial & Account Setup**

- The new practice will automatically begin a **7-day free trial** under the corporate account.
- Each practice is created as a **standalone account**, with:
  - Its own dashboard
  - Practice info section
  - Team member management
  - Billing information

**Account Independence & Control**

- Each practice operates **independently** while remaining under the corporate umbrella.
- Corporate account holders retain central oversight of all practices.
- Each practice is assigned a **unique Practice ID**, used to:
  - Connect devices
  - Manage content
- **Playlists are not shared** between practices, ensuring each location has a customized video lineup.

\
**_Key Notes_**

- Adding a practice does not affect existing accounts.
- Corporate oversight ensures visibility across all practices.
- Each practice can manage its own billing independently, but the corporate account maintains overall control.
