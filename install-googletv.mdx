---
title: "Installing and Connecting Channel D on Chromecast with Google TV / Google TV Smart TVs"
---

✅**Step 1: Check Compatibility**

- This guide supports:
  - **Chromecast with Google TV (HD or 4K)**
  - **Smart TVs with Google TV** (Sony, TCL, Hisense, Philips, Sharp)
  - **Android TVs** (slightly different interface but same process)

✅**Step 2: Plug in Chromecast Device**

- Plug the **Google TV dongle** into an HDMI port on your TV.
- Connect the **USB cable to power**, using the **included power adapter**.
  - ⚠️ **Do not plug into the TV’s USB port**—it may not provide enough power.

✅**Step 3: Use the Chromecast Remote**

- Use the **remote control** that came with the Chromecast.
- Press and hold the **pairing buttons** on the remote.
- Select your **language** when prompted.

✅**Step 4: Connect to Wi-Fi & Google Account**

- If you have an Android phone:
  - You can **transfer Wi-Fi and Google account** directly.
- **If not:**
  - Manually select your **Wi-Fi network**, enter the **password**, and **sign in to your Google account**.

✅**Step 5: Accept Prompts**

- Follow the prompts to:
  - **Accept terms**
  - Click **"Yes"**, **"Continue"**, **"No Thanks"**, and agree where needed
  - If checkboxes appear, select them; if not, continue

**<u>Installing Channel D</u>**

✅**Step 6: Access the App Store**

- Go to the **home screen**, locate the **“Apps” section**.
- Click on **Google Play Store** (or search icon if not visible).

✅**Step 7: Search and Install Channel D** 

- Search for **“Channel D”** (use the voice feature or type it in).
- Select and **install** the app.
