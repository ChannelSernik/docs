## **Where You Can Upload Your Logo**

You can upload your practice logo from **three different areas** within your Channel D dashboard:

### **1. Upload from the Practice/Dashboard (Top Right)**

In the **Practice/Dashboard** tab, click the **Upload Logo** button located in the **top-right corner** of the page.

### **2. Upload from the Other Photos Section**

Still within the **Practice/Dashboard** tab, scroll down to the **Other Photos** section.\
Locate the **Upload Logo** button here to add or replace your logo.

### **3. Upload from the Practice/Settings Tab**

Go to the **Practice/Settings** tab.\
Click the **Upload Logo** button located at the **top-left** of the page.

## **Important Notes**

### **✔ Use a High-Quality Image**

For best results in your custom videos, ensure you upload a **clear, high-resolution logo**.

### **✔ How Logo Updates Affect Your Videos**

If you upload a new logo, **all existing videos that feature your previous logo will automatically move from “Now Playing” to “In Production.”**\
Our team will update these videos with the new logo and return them to **Now Playing** once complete.
