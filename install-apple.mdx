## **Initial Apple TV Setup**

### **Step 1: Start Setup**

- Turn on your Apple TV.
- Select your **preferred language**.

### **Step 2: Choose Setup Method**

- If you have an **iPhone or iPad**, select **“Set Up with Device.”**
- Otherwise, choose **“Set Up Manually.”**

### **Step 3: Connect to Wi-Fi**

- Select your **practice Wi-Fi network**.
- Enter the **Wi-Fi password**.

### **Step 4: Sign In with Apple ID**

- Enter your **Apple ID** or create a new account.
- Enter the **verification code** sent to your device.
- Once verified, you’ll be added as a user.

### **Step 5: Enable Basic Settings**

- Enable **Location Services** and **Analytics**.
- Set password settings to **“Require Password for All Purchases.”**
- On the purchase prompt, click **“Not Now.”**
- Ensure the device is fully activated.

### **Step 6: Label the Device (Optional)**

You can label each Apple TV for easy room identification, such as:

- Patient Lounge
- Treatment Room
- Hygiene Room

---

## **Install and Launch Channel D**

### **Step 7: Open the App Store**

- Go to the **Apple TV Home Screen**.
- Open the **App Store**.

### **Step 8: Search and Install Channel D**

- Select the **Search** bar.
- Type **“Channel D”** (ensure there's a space between “Channel” and “D”).
- Select **Channel D** from the results.
- After installation, click **Open**.

### **Step 9: Enter Practice ID**

- When prompted, enter your **6-digit Practice ID**\
  (found at the top left of your Channel D dashboard on your computer).

### **Step 10: Confirm Video Playback**

- Click **Connect**.
- Your selected Channel D videos will now begin playing.

### **Step 11: Locate the App on Home Screen**

- The Channel D app will now be available on your **Apple TV Home Screen**.

---

## **Prevent Screensaver Interruptions**

### **Step 12: Disable Screensaver**

- Go to: **Settings \> General \> Screensaver**
- Set **“Start After”** to **Never**.

### **Step 13: Why Disable the Screensaver?**

- Prevents Channel D video playback from stopping.
- Ensures continuous streaming during office hours.

---

## **Troubleshooting**

### **Step 14: App Updates**

- Channel D normally updates **automatically** from the App Store.

### **Step 15: Reinstall If Needed**

If an update fails or becomes corrupted:

- Select the Channel D icon.
- Press and hold the **center button** until options appear.
- Choose **Delete** to uninstall.
- Reinstall by searching and downloading the app again.
