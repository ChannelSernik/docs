## **Roku Setup and Pairing**

### **Step 1: Initial Roku Setup**

- Select your **language**.
- Connect to your **Wi-Fi network** and enter the password.
- Allow the device to complete any **system updates**.

### **Step 2: Pair Roku Remote and Control TV**

- Use the Roku remote to sync with your TV.
- Enter your **TV brand** when prompted.
- Follow the on-screen instructions to calibrate **remote control and volume**.

### **Step 3: Activate Roku Device**

- Create or log in to your **Roku account**.
- Enter your **email address** when prompted.
- Check your email and complete the activation process.

---

## **Installing Channel D**

### **Step 4: Access Roku Home Screen**

- After activation, you will be brought to the **Roku Home Screen**.

### **Step 5: Search for Channel D**

- Select **Streaming Channels**.
- Use the search bar to type **“Channel D”**\
  _(ensure there is a space before the “D”)_.

### **Step 6: Add and Launch Channel D**

- Select the **Channel D** thumbnail from the results.
- Click **Add Channel** to install.
- Open the **Channel D** app.

### **Step 7: Enter Practice ID**

- Enter your **6-digit Practice ID**.\
  (Found in the top-left corner of your Channel D dashboard on your computer.)

### **Step 8: Verify Content Display**

- Your Roku device will now play the videos selected in your Channel D dashboard.

---

## **Prevent Screensaver and Power Save Mode Interruptions**

### **Step 9: Disable Screensaver Settings**

Go to:\
**Settings \> Theme \> Theme Packs**

- Turn off **wallpaper animations**.
- Disable the **screensaver**.

### **Step 10: Turn Off Auto Power Savings**

Go to:\
**Settings \> System \> Power \> Auto Power Savings**

- Uncheck **“Auto power off.”**
- Press **OK** once it shows unchecked.

---

## **Troubleshooting**

### **Step 11: Check for Updates**

- From the Home screen, select **Channel D**.
- Roku will automatically check for **app updates**.
- Let the updates complete to maintain smooth performance.

### **Step 12: Reinstall If Needed**

If an update is interrupted or corrupted:

- Highlight **Channel D**.
- Select **Remove Channel**.
- Reinstall by searching **“Channel D”** again in the app store.
