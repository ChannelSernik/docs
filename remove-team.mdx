---
title: "Removing a Team Member in Channel D"
description: "This guide explains how to remove a team member from your Channel D practice dashboard, and outlines what happens to videos they were assigned to after removal."
---

### **Step 1: Navigate to the Team Member List**

- Go to the **Practice / Dashboard** tab on your Channel D account.
- Locate the **team member** you want to remove from the active list.

### **Step 2: Open the Edit Menu**

- Click on the team member’s name to open their profile options.
- Select **“Edit Team Member”** to access the editing popup.

### **Step 3: Remove the Team Member**

- Scroll to the bottom of the popup window.
- Click **“Remove Team Member”**.
- Confirm the removal if prompted.

### **Step 4: Understand the Impact**

- Any videos the removed team member appeared in will be **removed from the “Now Playing” column**.
- These videos will move to the **“Needs Attention” column**, awaiting reassignment.
- You will need to **add another team member** to those videos for them to resume playback in the queue.
