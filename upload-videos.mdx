---

**ProcedurePart A: Uploading a Video**

**Step 1: Log In**

1. Open a browser (Google Chrome recommended).
2. Go to the **Channel D website** and sign in with your **email address** and **password**.\
   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa675fd073a4f29f61957.jpeg" alt="Channel D login page" /\>
3. Once signed in, you will land on your **dashboard**.

**Step 2: Navigate to Uploads**

1. From the left-hand navigation menu, click **Uploads**.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa6f7aaad916c4278c9f4.jpeg" alt="Channel D uploads page" /\>
1. You will be redirected to the **Uploads Page**, which displays all previously uploaded videos.

   \<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/msgsndr/YzcFQ6sDBPZ4Z64nJ8hp/media/692fa746aaad916a3978eb19.jpeg" alt="Channel D uploads page" /\>

**Step 3: Start Uploading**

1. In the **upper-right corner** of the Uploads page, click **Upload Media**.
2. From the pop-up options, select **Upload a Video**.
