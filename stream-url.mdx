**Access Your Website Account:**

Log in to your website hosting or account management platform. This could be a web hosting control panel (e.g., cPanel), a website builder, a content management system (CMS), or an e-commerce platform like Shopify. The exact steps may vary depending on your hosting provider.

**Navigate to Website Settings:**

Once you are logged in, look for an option or menu related to website settings or customization. This may be labeled as "Settings," "Customization," "Appearance," "Design," or something similar. Click on it to access your website's customization options.

**Find the Logo Section:**

Within the customization or design settings, there should be a section specifically for uploading your logo. This section might be labeled as "Logo," "Site Identity," "Header," or similar. Click on it to open the logo upload interface.

**Upload Your Logo:**

In the logo upload section, you will typically see an option to upload your logo image file. Click on this option, and a file upload dialog will appear.

**Select and Upload Your Logo File:**

Click the "Upload" or "Choose File" button (the wording may vary), and then select the logo image file from your computer. Ensure that the image file meets the recommended specifications for size and format.

**Adjust Logo Settings (if necessary):**

Depending on your website platform, you may have the option to adjust the logo's size, alignment, and other display settings. Follow the on-screen instructions or settings to make any necessary adjustments.

1. **Save Your Changes:**

After you've uploaded your logo and made any desired adjustments, be sure to save your changes. There may be a "Save," "Update," or "Publish" button or option. Click it to confirm your changes.

**Preview Your Website:**

To ensure that your logo looks as expected on your website, you can preview your site in a new browser tab or window.

**Publish Your Changes (if needed):**

In some cases, you may need to publish or apply your changes for them to appear on your live website. Follow the instructions provided by your platform to do so.

**Check Your Website:**

Finally, visit your website in a web browser to verify that the logo has been successfully uploaded and is displayed correctly.
